PERSONAL STATEMENT

As a Scientist with a Ph.D. in gut microbial biotechnology from ETH Zurich, I bring 7 years of research experience to the pharmaceutical industry. I possess in-depth knowledge of complex biological systems and extensive hands-on experience in protocol development, molecular and analytical methods. My strong track record includes executing experiments from design to data analysis, strengthened by effective collaboration skills developed through work in international research teams. As a published researcher, I excel in communication and am proficient in advanced data analysis and bioinformatics tools. My goal is to apply my multidisciplinary expertise to transform scientific insights into innovative pharmaceutical solutions.
